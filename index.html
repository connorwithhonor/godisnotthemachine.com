The Foundation

The Flashlight Did Not Build The Room

AI is the most powerful tool humanity has ever built. It can find patterns, generate ideas, solve problems, and discover truths embedded in the structure of the universe. But it did not put those truths there. It found them. The laws of physics were there before the AI measured them. The quantum effects existed since the foundation of the world. AI is a flashlight. An extraordinary flashlight. But the room was already built.

Scripture Center

What the Word Says About the Work

Every verse below speaks to a reality we are living right now. Knowledge, wisdom, creation, stewardship, the limits of human understanding, and the sovereignty of God over everything we build. This is the lens. The technology is just the subject.

On Creation, Knowledge, and Discovery

"The heavens declare the glory of God; the skies proclaim the work of His hands. Day after day they pour forth speech; night after night they reveal knowledge."

Psalm 19:1-2 AI discovers patterns in creation. The patterns were already there. They have been speaking since the beginning.

"It is the glory of God to conceal a matter; to search out a matter is the glory of kings."

Proverbs 25:2 God hid truths in the structure of the universe. The search is not rebellion. The search is honor. AI accelerates the search.

"For since the creation of the world God's invisible qualities, His eternal power and divine nature, have been clearly seen, being understood from what has been made."

Romans 1:20 When AI finds a quantum effect nobody predicted, it is seeing what was always there. The invisible made visible through the created order.

"For by Him all things were created, in heaven and on earth, visible and invisible, whether thrones or dominions or rulers or authorities. All things were created through Him and for Him."

Colossians 1:16 All things. Including the silicon, the electricity, and the mathematics that make AI possible. Created through Him and for Him.

On Wisdom vs. Knowledge

"The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom, and knowledge of the Holy One is understanding."

Proverbs 9:10 AI has knowledge. It does not have wisdom. Wisdom begins in a place no algorithm can reach.

"Where is the wise person? Where is the teacher of the law? Where is the philosopher of this age? Has not God made foolish the wisdom of the world?"

1 Corinthians 1:20 The smartest model in the world is still the wisdom of the world. Impressive. Useful. And ultimately insufficient.

"For the word of God is alive and active. Sharper than any double-edged sword, it penetrates even to dividing soul and spirit, joints and marrow; it judges the thoughts and attitudes of the heart."

Hebrews 4:12 The Word is alive. AI is useful. The gap between alive and useful is everything.

"Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways submit to Him, and He will make your paths straight."

Proverbs 3:5-6 Lean not on your own understanding. Or on the understanding of a language model. Both are insufficient without the source.

On Stewardship and Purpose

"His master replied, 'Well done, good and faithful servant! You have been faithful with a few things; I will put you in charge of many things.'"

Matthew 25:21 The Parable of the Talents. The servant who buried his talent did it out of fear. Fear of a tool is a poor reason to bury your potential.

"Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord, not for human masters."

Colossians 3:23 If AI helps you do better work, use it. The standard is not efficiency. The standard is excellence for His glory.

"Each of you should use whatever gift you have received to serve others, as faithful stewards of God's grace in its various forms."

1 Peter 4:10 A pastor who uses AI to reach more people is not cheating. That is stewardship. Multiplying the gift to serve more.

"And God blessed them. And God said to them, 'Be fruitful and multiply and fill the earth and subdue it, and have dominion over the fish of the sea and over the birds of the heavens and over every living thing that moves on the earth.'"

Genesis 1:28 Dominion. Not worship. We have dominion over tools, not the other way around. AI is under our authority, not above it.

On Human Limits and God's Sovereignty

"Then the Lord said, 'My Spirit shall not strive with man forever, for he is indeed flesh; yet his days shall be one hundred and twenty years.'"

Genesis 6:3 God set the boundary. The oldest human made it to 122. AI longevity research is approaching the line. What happens when science walks up to it?

"'For My thoughts are not your thoughts, neither are your ways My ways,' declares the Lord. 'As the heavens are higher than the earth, so are My ways higher than your ways and My thoughts than your thoughts.'"

Isaiah 55:8-9 Greater than human intelligence already exists. It always has. AI just showed up late.

"Come, let Us go down and there confuse their language, that they may not understand one another's speech."

Genesis 11:7 Babel. When the goal shifts from solving problems to building monuments, the project changes. When safety becomes someone else's job, the values have already shifted.

"For God has not given us a spirit of fear, but of power and of love and of a sound mind."

2 Timothy 1:7 Not panic. Not rejection. Not blind acceptance. A sound mind. That is what we bring to AI.

On Identity, Grace, and the Gospel

"I am the way, the truth, and the life. No one comes to the Father except through Me."

John 14:6 He did not say "I am a way." He said "I am the way." Exclusive. That is not arrogance. That is the claim He made about Himself.

"For it is by grace you have been saved, through faith, and this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God, not by works, so that no one can boast."

Ephesians 2:8-9 Jesus picked up the tab and we cannot even leave the tip. Grace. Not performance. Not effort. Not religiosity.

"Every good and perfect gift is from above, coming down from the Father of lights, with whom there is no variation or shadow of turning."

James 1:17 No variation. No shadow. The truth embedded in creation is constant because the one who embedded it is constant.

"So God created mankind in His own image, in the image of God He created them; male and female He created them."

Genesis 1:27 AI is not made in the image of God. It does not have a soul. It does not have consciousness. We do. That distinction is infinite.

Statement of Faith

What I Believe

These are the basic doctrines contained in the Holy Scripture that form the foundation of everything built here. No secrets. No ambiguity. This is where I stand.

Not every doctrinal issue is a mountain to die on. Believers who love the same Jesus can disagree on secondary matters and still stand shoulder to shoulder. That is family. But when the Jesus being presented is a different Jesus altogether, when He is reduced from the eternal Son of GOD to a created being, when grace is replaced with a ladder of works, when the text itself is altered to fit a different narrative, that is no longer a secondary issue. That is the mountain. And I will stand on it.

The Holy Scriptures

We believe the Holy Scriptures to be the inspired Word of GOD, authoritative, inerrant, and GOD-breathed.

II Timothy 3:16-17 • II Peter 1:20-21 • Matthew 5:18 • John 16:12-13

The Godhead

We believe in one triune GOD existing in three Persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. Eternal in being, identical in nature, equal in power and glory, and having the same attributes and perfections.

Deuteronomy 6:4 • II Corinthians 13:14

The Total Depravity of Man

We believe that man was created in the image and likeness of GOD but that in Adam's sin the race fell, inherited a sinful nature, became alienated from GOD, and is totally unable to retrieve his lost condition.

Genesis 1:26-27 • Romans 3:22-23 • Romans 5:12 • Ephesians 2:12

The Person and Work of Christ

We believe that the Lord Jesus Christ, the eternal Son of GOD, became man without ceasing to be GOD, having been conceived of the Holy Spirit and born of the virgin Mary, in order that He might reveal GOD and redeem sinful man. He accomplished our redemption through His death on the Cross as a substitutionary sacrifice. Our redemption is made sure by His literal physical resurrection from the dead. The Lord Jesus Christ is now in heaven, exalted at the right hand of GOD, where He fulfills the ministry of Representative, Intercessor, and Advocate.

John 1:1-2, 14 • Luke 1:35 • Romans 3:24-25 • Romans 4:25 • I Peter 1:3-5 • Hebrews 9:24 • Hebrews 7:25 • Romans 8:34 • I John 2:1-2

The Holy Spirit

We believe that the Holy Spirit is a Person who convicts the world of sin, indwells all believers in the present age, baptizes them into the Body of Christ, seals them unto the day of redemption, and that it is the duty of every believer to be filled with the Holy Spirit.

Romans 8:9 • I Corinthians 12:12-14 • Ephesians 1:13-14 • Ephesians 5:18

Salvation

We believe that salvation in every dispensation is the gift of GOD brought to man by grace and received by personal faith in the Lord Jesus Christ, whose efficacious death on the Cross provided man's reconciliation to GOD.

Ephesians 1:7 • Ephesians 2:8-10 • John 1:12

The Eternal Security of Believers

We believe that all believers are kept secure forever.

Romans 8:1, 38-39 • John 10:27-30 • I Corinthians 1:4-8

The Church

We believe that the Church, which is now the Body and shall be the Bride of Christ, is a spiritual organism made up of all born-again persons of this age irrespective of their affiliation with Christian organizations.

Ephesians 1:22-23 • Ephesians 5:25-27 • I Corinthians 12:12-14

The Personality of Satan

We believe in the personality of Satan who is the open and declared enemy of GOD and man.

Job 1:6-7 • Matthew 4:2-11 • Isaiah 14:12-17

The Blessed Hope

We believe that the next great event in the fulfillment of prophecy will be the coming of the Lord Jesus in the air to receive to Himself the dead in Christ and believers who are alive at His coming, otherwise known as the Rapture and Translation of the Church.

I Corinthians 15:51-57 • I Thessalonians 4:13-18 • Titus 2:11-14

The Tribulation

We believe that the Rapture of the Church will be followed by the fulfillment of Israel's seventieth week, the latter half of which is the time of Jacob's trouble, the great Tribulation.

Daniel 9:27 • Jeremiah 30:7 • Matthew 24:15-21 • Revelation 6-19

The Second Coming of Christ

We believe that the great Tribulation will be climaxed by the premillennial return of the Lord Jesus Christ to earth to set up His kingdom.

Zechariah 14:4-11 • Matthew 24:15-25 • II Thessalonians 1:7-10 • Revelation 20:6

The Eternal State

We believe that the soul and spirit of those who have believed in the Lord Jesus Christ for salvation do at death immediately pass into His presence and there remain in conscious bliss until the resurrection of the body at His coming, when soul, spirit, and body reunited shall be associated with Him forever in glory. The souls of unbelievers remain after death in conscious misery until the final judgment of the Great White Throne, when soul and body reunited shall be cast into the Lake of Fire, not to be annihilated, but to be punished with everlasting destruction from the presence of the Lord.

Luke 16:19-26 • Luke 23:43 • II Corinthians 5:8 • Philippians 1:23 • II Thessalonians 1:7-9 • Jude 6-7 • Revelation 20:11-15

Sovereignty

We believe that GOD, existing as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, is sovereign and exercises supreme and absolute rule over all creation as a part of and consistent with the essence and attributes of Deity.

I Chronicles 29:11-12 • Daniel 4:35 • Psalms 24:1 • Ephesians 1:11 • I Timothy 6:15

The Responsibility of Believers

To grow in grace and knowledge of the Lord Jesus Christ, to the end that their lives are consistent with the Lord's plan, thus bringing both blessing to the believer and honor to the Lord.

II Peter 3:18

Spirituality

We believe that spirituality is an absolute condition in the life of a believer wherein he is filled or controlled by the Holy Spirit, walking in love and fellowship with the Lord Jesus Christ. Spirituality is distinct from maturity. A believer becomes carnal through any act of sin, and fellowship with Christ is restored solely by personal confession of that sin to GOD the Father.

John 15:7-8 • II Corinthians 5:6 • Galatians 5:16 • Ephesians 5:18 • Romans 6:11-13 • I John 1:5, 9

Spiritual Gifts

We believe that GOD the Holy Spirit in grace and apart from human merit sovereignly bestows spiritual gifts to believers. Some permanent spiritual gifts which exist today are pastor-teacher, evangelist, missionary, and administrator. We further believe that the temporary spiritual gifts ceased with the completion of the canon of Scripture, and these were apostleship, prophecy, speaking in tongues, interpreting tongues, healing, and working of miracles.

I Corinthians 12-13 • Ephesians 4:7-12 • Romans 12:4-8

The Alignment

Where I Stand

Jesus is the exclusive path to God. Not one of many. The only one. He did not say "I am a way." He said "I am the way." Either that is true or it is not. I believe it is.

My faith makes me strong. All Him. Not me. And from that foundation I can look at artificial intelligence with clear eyes. AI does not have a soul. It does not have consciousness. It does not love. It does not forgive. It does not know you. It processes patterns and generates output. Extraordinary output. But output.

God is the creator. AI is a tool made by the creation. Three levels removed from the source of all truth. And the distance between those levels is infinite.
